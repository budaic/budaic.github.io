IPhO Preparation Camp Organization

I was one of the chief organizers of the first Hungarian International Physics Olympiad preparation camp "Fagypont" in the January of 2024. Since then, we have continued the camps, preparing students for the national and international rounds.

The Camps

One evening at the 2023 IPhO in Tokyo, we were discussing our plans for the next year with my team members. We had heard from others about their national preparation camps, and we decided we would start the tradition in Hungary as well. The first camp "Fagypont" was held in January 2024, and it was a great success. We had almost 30 students, and had great fun. I am proud, that that year 4 out of the 5 Hungarian team members were from our camp.
